Robots

What does it do?

A robot Is a machine that you can give anything from simple instructions to far more complex ones They are programmable by a computer system and able to function autonomously, by an external device or even via a device embedded within. Robots can be autonomous or semi-autonomous as stated above this means that robots are currently being used in many different industries. anything from industrial bots, medical operating robots, collectively programmed swarm bots or even a ping pong robot! Another plus about robots being in the field, is the fact that they make high danger jobs far less hazardous for humans. For e.g. the manufacturing process can cause harm in many ways a task as simple as directing and picking up boxes can over time cause harm in your back. But not a robots in fact very repetitive tasks are where there shine the most they allow for a much higher accuracy/base line level of consistency. What is the Likely Impact?

Lets expand on the loss of jobs, again it is a double edged sword. Robots can be far superior in most industries and long term can be cheaper than humans. upkeep and production cost will become cheaper as technology progresses. The future robots will be able to perform simple task for more accurately and constant then humans. You wont have to worry about paying for workers breaks or stressing over the manual handling risk of a human worker. Insurance cost will drop., though with that the power cost and supplier cost will rise what seems to be infinitely. This in my opinion isn’t a completely bad thing. It will force people into learning a specialty of their own improving the general mass of intelligence. Sadly though it will flush out the few who cant afford a working robot and cause many business to fail as robot influence business production speeds will rise. Businesses like Farming, delivery/warehouses and even something as small as a grocery store will be influenced. Again this could ruin an industry and bring it to its knees. It would be wild if robots begin to autonomously write pieces of complex code to fix a nation wide problem. Again though I think that would also force people to stress about bigger problems like how to incorporate robots/ automated workers in an industry like manufacturing and mining. In return bringing the cost of resources down. Making it easier for construction(Prices would change so much over time).
